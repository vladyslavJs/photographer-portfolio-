A few word about me.

Hello! My name is Ira, I'm 26, and photography is not just my job, it's a true passion. I love capturing emotions, the play of light and shadow, and creating shots that tell stories. For me, every photoshoot is a unique process where I strive to convey the beauty of the moment and the individuality of each person. Creativity is my life, and I am always on the lookout for new ideas and inspiration. Let's create something beautiful together!

PHOTOGRAPHY. It gives me immense strength and opens my eyes wider with each passing day. I photograph people, and it doesn't matter who is standing in front of me. To come to my photoshoot, you don't need to be a model or have any experience. I often work with people who are seeing themselves through someone else's eyes for the first time, and this is a very interesting process for me. With me, you will not feel discomfort or tension. Our entire process will be easy and pleasant. I always guide you, help you open up, and do everything to achieve powerful results. The philosophy of my work is naturalness, ease, and simplicity. I capture people without masks. I photograph them as real, alive, and authentic as they are. It's very important for me that we are close in spirit, that we see the world in similar ways, and value our naturalness and individuality. For me, the perfect shot is when I truly believe in you. I believe that you really laughed wholeheartedly or cried from happiness. It doesn't matter to me what your body type is, how old you are, or whether you have experience. I photograph everyone who resonates with my approach and worldview. If that's you, I'm waiting for you! Come and join me!

Cost of services

When you come to me for a photoshoot, you seek my vision. I work in minimalist studios and choose natural tones in clothing. I shoot without makeup or with minimal use of it. To achieve the best result, we discuss all details together, based on your preferences and my style. Your trust and willingness to collaborate at all stages of the shoot are extremely important

Individual Photoshoot

  • consultation before the shoot, discussion of your request, assistance with posing and location selection
  • duration of the shoot — 1/1.5 hours
  • 25 photos with color correction and retouching
  • processing time — 1-2 weeks
  • during the shoot, we'll manage to capture 2-3 outfits

Price: 250 zł
